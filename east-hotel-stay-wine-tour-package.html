Stay and winery tour packages

Taste more than relaxation when you combine a luxury Canberra winery tour with a stay at the elegant boutique East Hotel.

Canberra is the perfect place to delve into the local wine region, soak up the flavour and scenery and relax in an iconic boutique hotel that fosters the importance of all the little things. Combine a bespoke, selective, private wine tour with a genuine, elegant, boutique hotel for a perfect weekend away.

Wine and stay packages will start from $575 for two people to experience a half-day bespoke wine tour visiting 3 wineries with lunch and a night’s stay in a Luxe Studio at East Hotel. Van Du Vin tours tailor their tours based on guests’ wine preferences, so prepare yourself for a truly great experience.

About East Hotel

East Hotel is a contemporary, cool and vibrant apartment hotel offering visitors to Canberra, whether overnighters, business guests, long stay guests or families, a unique Canberra experience. A big part of this local experience is the warm and casually attentive customer service.

Situated between the vibrant shopping and restaurant hubs of Kingston and Manuka in Canberra’s prestigious “Inner South”, East is the ultimate location for your visit to the capital.

To learn more about East Hotel visit their website.

Your stay and tour package will include:

  • Overnight accommodation for 2 in a Luxe studio room (longer stays and upgrades available)
  • 3 wine tasting stops at cellar doors in the Canberra wine region (upgrades to a full-day tour available)
  • Wood fire pizza lunch. Upgrades to multicourse restaurant lunch available
  • Transport in clean and modern air-conditioned vehicles
  • Pickup and dropoff from East Hotel
  • Friendly, professional and Canberra food and wine obsessed tour guide and driver
  • Refrigerated cold drinks available in the vehicle

Sample itinerary for morning half-day tour

  • 10.00am - Depart East Hotel
  • 10.30am - Gallagher Wines wine tasting with matching house made cheese and pickles
  • 11.30am - Four winds wine tasting and wood fire pizza lunch
  • 12.30pm - McKellar Ridge wine tasting
  • Arrive back at East Hotel 1.45pm

All our tours are private tours. You won't be mixed with other groups. We will always try to tailor tours to include wineries you would like to visit, or we can create a tour tailored to a specific type of wine you prefer. Just let us know during the booking process what your preferences are if you have any.
