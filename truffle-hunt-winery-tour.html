The ultimate Canberra winery and truffle tour

Private full-day gourmet Canberra winery tour including a Truffle Farm truffle hunt and truffle degustation lunch.

About the truffle hunt and degustation lunch

Join Jayson and Australia's best truffle dog team on an exhilarating truffle hunt experience. Jayson will expertly guide you through everything you've ever wanted to know about truffles. Feel the rush of excitement as you join the hunt for the elusive black truffle through the forest of 6,000 oak and hazelnut trees.

Once the perfect truffle is found, we head back to the warmth of the truffle shed for a six-course degustation. The truffle dishes are prepared in front of you while teaching you all the secrets to using truffles at home. Sit back and enjoy the dishes or get up and get involved in this interactive and entertaining event. Please see our FAQ page for more.

To learn more about the Truffle Hunt visit The Truffle Farm

Your tour will include:

  • Truffle dog truffle hunt
  • Demonstrations on how to best use truffles in cooking
  • 6 course truffle inspired degustation lunch
  • 3 wine tasting stops at cellar doors in the Canberra wine region
  • Transport in clean and modern air-conditioned vehicles
  • Pickup and drop-off to your choice of location in the Canberra region
  • Friendly, professional and Canberra food and wine obsessed tour guide and driver
  • Refrigerated cold drinks available in the vehicle

Sample itinerary for

  • 9.00am - Depart your choice of location in the Canberra region
  • 9.30am - Truffle Farm truffle introduction
  • 10.00am - Truffle hunt with the truffle dog team
  • 11.00am - Truffle degustation lunch and wine
  • 1.30pm - Mckellar Ridge wine tasting and winery tour
  • 2.30pm - Gallagher Wines wine tasting with matching house made cheese and pickles
  • 3.30pm - Brindabella Hills wine tasting
  • Arrive back in Canberra 5.00pm

All our tours are private tours. You won't be mixed with other groups. However, the experience at the Truffle farm is mixed with other guests. We can always try to tailor tours to include wineries you would like to visit, or we can create a tour tailored to a specific type of wine you prefer. Just let us know during the booking process what your preferences are if you have any.

Cancellation policy

This is a partner product and tour purchase is bound by the cancellation policies of The Truffle Farm.

Guests may request a change of date after they have made a booking. This is based on the availability of tickets on the preferred date. All changes incur an AUD $25 booking fee. This request must be made 21 days prior to the original booking date.

Tickets to this event are non-refundable.

If you cancel this booking The Truffle Farm will charge VAN DU VIN $225 per person. A fee of $225 will be passed on to any VAN DU VIN customer tour bookings seeking a refund as well as any standard VAN DU VIN cancellation fees.
