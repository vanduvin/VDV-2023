Canberra winery tour packages

Bonjour! Van Du Vin provide a range of winery tours for the Canberra region.

Our Petit tour is great if you're short on time, while our Tour and Luxe tours offer a great opportunity to really experience the best of what Canberra wineries have to offer.

All our set tour packages are private small group tours, tailored to get the best out of the seasons and who is producing the best wine and food that we think you will enjoy.

Our Canberra winery tours packages are for a maximum of 11 guests and the price includes all wine tasting fees, food and transport.
