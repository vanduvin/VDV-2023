Murrumbateman winery tours

Canberra’s wine region including Murrumbateman and the Lake George region is home to 140 vineyards and 40 cellar doors. The majority are within 35 minutes’ drive of the CBD. With an emphasis on producing quality cool-climate wines, the Canberra wine region around Murrumbateman is a must do for locals and visitors.

The region is well known for it's Shiraz (or Syrah), Riesling and Viognier - but you'll be delighted by the quality of the cool-climate Pinot Noir, Pinot Gris, Chardonnay and Tempranillo.

If you have a variety of wine you prefer, let us know when booking and we can tailor a tour to suit you.

Discover the best local wine and food

Canberra has been steadilly cementing itself as one of the best food and wine destinations in Australia. And when it comes to cool climate wines, Canberra has been taking on the world.

The beauty of Canberra as a growing food and wine destination, is how the local community support each other and share a common goal for providing great, sustainable products now and in the future.

Van Du Vin are looking forward to partnerning with a number of local businesses and we are excited to share them with you.
