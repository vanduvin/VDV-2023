Gift vouchers for Canberra winery tours

Van Du Vin offers a range of gift vouchers to use to explore Canberra region wineries on a winery tour.

Gift the ones you love with the gift of discovering some amazing wines on our winery tours Canberra.

Simply choose the amount you wish to gift and go through the checkout process. You can choose to automatically send the voucher to your recipients or keep it private if it's a surprise.

Our $600, $750 and $900 vouchers are the perfect amount to gift a couple our half-day, full-day and luxury tours respectively.

Vouchers are valid for 3 years from date of purchase. Gift cards can't be used with our partner packages. Multiple vouchers can be used for whole or part of a tour. Gift vouchers can not be refunded.

Custom winery tour experiences

We specialise in smaller, intimate, custom and luxurious tours. However, we can work with you if you have a larger group. You can also include Van Du Vin as part of an event or special dinner.
We will do our best to accommodate your requests and create a memorable experience.
